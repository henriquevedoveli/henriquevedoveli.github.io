Image Processing

1.1 Introduction to Image Processing

The text discusses image enhancement in digital image processing, which aims to improve the visibility, clarity, and overall quality of an image. It addresses issues like low contrast, poor brightness, noise, and blurriness. Image enhancement has applications in photography, surveillance, remote sensing, medical imaging, and more. The techniques of image enhancement covered include histogram equalization, contrast stretching, spatial filtering, frequency domain techniques, histogram specification, and adaptive enhancement. These techniques have diverse applications and benefits in different fields, such as medical imaging, satellite imagery, forensics, and entertainment. The significance of image enhancement lies in its ability to provide clearer and more informative images for various practical purposes. As technology advances, we can expect even more sophisticated image enhancement methods to be developed, further enhancing visual experiences and enabling critical applications across various domains.
